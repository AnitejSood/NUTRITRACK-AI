# NutriTrack AI — Quick User Guide

Welcome to **NutriTrack AI**, your personal AI-powered nutrition coach optimized for Indian diets. Here is a simple guide explaining what each page and feature in the app does:

---

## 1. 🏠 Home (Today's Progress)
This is your main dashboard. It gives you a complete picture of your nutrition for the day:
*   **Calorie Hero Card:** Displays your total calories consumed today versus your daily goal, showing how many calories you have remaining.
*   **Macros & Water Rings:** Four circular rings that visually show your progress for **Protein**, **Carbs**, **Fat**, and **Water**.
*   **Detailed Macros:** Displays exact amounts of macronutrients eaten, what percentage of your target you've reached, and how much is remaining.
*   **Water Tracker:** Log your water intake in 250ml intervals (cups) to easily hit your daily hydration target.
*   **Today's Meals Timeline:** Lists every meal you have logged today chronologically, showing a calorie and macro breakdown for each entry.

---

## 2. 📊 Analytics
Track your consistency and trends over time:
*   **Today's Summary:** A quick-glance view of your total calorie, protein, carb, and water intake for the day.
*   **7-Day Goal Adherence:** Shows the percentage of days over the last week that you successfully met your Calorie, Protein, and Water targets.
*   **Trend Charts:** Interactive line charts displaying your daily intake trends for Calories, Protein, Carbs, Fat, and Water over the past 7 days, complete with a dotted line marking your target goal.

---

## 3. ✨ Insights (AI Coach)
Get personalised feedback on your nutrition:
*   **Today at a Glance:** A summary of your daily achievements, goals, and logged meals.
*   **Generate Insights:** Tap this button to let Gemini AI analyze your day's logs and generate four distinct analysis cards:
    *   **Summary:** An overall review of your diet's balance today.
    *   **Protein Analysis:** Details on whether you met your protein target and how it impacts your body.
    *   **Water Analysis:** Spans hydration patterns and recommendations.
    *   **Recommendations:** Actionable, practical suggestions for tomorrow (recommending healthy Indian foods like paneer, dahi, dal, or sprouts based on what you lacked).

---

## 4. 🧠 Ask AI (Meal Planner)
Don't know what to eat next? Let the AI suggest something:
*   **Remaining Today:** Shows your current remaining macro budget (Calories, Protein, Carbs, and Fat left for the day).
*   **Suggest a Meal:** Tap this button, and Gemini AI will recommend a realistic, budget-friendly North Indian meal (like chole chawal, roti-dal, paneer, or lassi) that perfectly fits your remaining targets.
*   **After This Meal Preview:** Shows a visual preview of your progress bars to demonstrate exactly how close you'll get to your daily goals if you eat the recommended meal.

---

## 5. ⚙️ Settings
Manage your account and preferences:
*   **Profile Stats:** View your height, weight, activity level, current goals, and auto-calculated **BMI (Body Mass Index)** with category (Normal, Overweight, etc.).
*   **Edit Nutrition Goals:** Tap "Edit" to customize your daily Calorie, Protein, Carb, Fat, and Water targets.
*   **Preferences:** Easily toggle between **Dark Mode** and **Light Mode**.
*   **Sign Out:** Safely log out of your account.

---

## ➕ How to Log a Meal (Natural Language)
From any page in the app, tap the floating pink **`+`** button in the bottom right corner:
1.  Select your meal type (**Breakfast**, **Lunch**, **Dinner**, or **Snacks**).
2.  Type what you ate in natural, everyday language (e.g., *"2 rotis and a bowl of dal"* or *"chole bhature with a glass of sweet lassi"*).
3.  Tap **Analyze with Gemini**. The AI will break down your description into individual foods, calculate their Indian nutrition values, and sum up the macros.
4.  Review the AI analysis and tap **Save Meal** to add it to your dashboard!
